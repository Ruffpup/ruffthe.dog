## Your gear works hard—learn how to take care of it.

**Pup Gear Care 101** is an accessible, hands-on class designed to help pups, handlers, and gear users confidently clean, store, and maintain their pup gear.

Drawing on **20 years of experience** in kink and pup play, **Ruff Pup** shares practical, harm-reduction-focused techniques that extend the life of gear while keeping pups safe, comfortable, and confident in play.

### What to Expect

This class is **shame-free, realistic, and designed for real life**—not perfection. We will cover:

- **Hygiene:** How to effectively clean leather, neoprene, and silicone.
- **Maintenance:** Conditioning and storage tips to prevent damage.
- **Safety:** Spotting wear and tear to ensure your gear is safe for scenes.

> "A well-cared-for hood is a happy hood."
