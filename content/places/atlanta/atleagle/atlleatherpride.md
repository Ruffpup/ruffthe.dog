## Welcome to Atlanta Leather Pride

Whether this is your very first leather event or you've been attending for decades, I'm glad our paths crossed.

Atlanta Leather Pride is a weekend dedicated to celebrating leather history, service, education, connection, and the people who make this community extraordinary. You'll find contests, vendors, classes, socials, demonstrations, bootblacking, and countless conversations that often become lasting friendships.

If you happened to tap my hand today, congratulations—you found one of my favorite little Easter eggs.

## Why am I bootblacking?

Bootblacking isn't just about making leather shiny.

It's an act of service.

Every pair of boots, every jacket, every vest, and every piece of leather tells a story. Caring for that gear is really about caring for the person wearing it and the experiences it's been through.

One of the things I love most about sitting behind the bootblack stand is that it gives people permission to slow down. A shine might start with polish and brushes, but it almost always turns into conversation. I've met first-time attendees, longtime leathermen, titleholders, curious newcomers, and people who simply wanted a quiet place to sit for a while.

Every shine is different.

Every story matters.
