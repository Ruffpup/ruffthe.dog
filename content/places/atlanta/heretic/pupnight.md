## What is Pup Night?

Pup Night at the Heretic is one of Atlanta’s regular gathering spots for the pup community.

It’s a night where pups, handlers, curious newcomers, and friends come together to socialize, dance, and enjoy being a little playful in public.

You’ll usually see:

- pups in full gear
- pups just wearing a hood or collar
- handlers and friends hanging out
- plenty of tail wags and chaos energy on the dance floor

It’s part bar night, part community meetup, and part playground for the pup side of ourselves.

If you scanned my hand tonight, you definitely found the chaos corgi.
