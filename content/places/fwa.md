# You met Ruff at FWA 🐾

Well hello there.

If you’re reading this, something fun just happened.

You tapped your phone to my hand and ended up here.

Yes, the chip is real.  
And no, your phone didn’t break.

You just scanned one of the NFC implants in my hand.

Most people check their phone…  
Then look at my hand…  
Then check their phone again.

Welcome to Ruff’s corner of the internet.

## Ruff at Furry Weekend Atlanta

FWA is one of my favorite weekends of the year.

Thousands of furries from around the world show up in Atlanta for a few days of art, community, costumes, parties, and a whole lot of chaos in the best possible way.

If we met here, chances are we crossed paths somewhere like:

- the dealer’s den  
- a hallway full of fursuiters  
- a late night party  
- the convention lobby  
- or somewhere completely random

That’s kind of the magic of FWA.

## So why does my hand scan?

Because meeting someone should be memorable.

The chip you scanned is a tiny NFC implant. Your phone briefly powered it and it sent you to this page.

No batteries.  
No tracking.  
Just a little bit of technology and a fun way to say hello.

And honestly, it’s a pretty good conversation starter.

## If we met this weekend

I’m glad you said hi.

One of the best parts of conventions like FWA is how easy it is to meet people you never would have crossed paths with otherwise.

Sometimes those moments turn into friendships that last for years.

Sometimes they just turn into a great story.

Either way, I’m glad you’re part of it.

## One last thing

If you see me again somewhere around the con, come say hi.
