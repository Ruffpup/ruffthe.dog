## What Is Dog Days?

Dog Days is one of the signature events hosted by the **Southeast Pet Play Association (SEPA)**.

For a few days each year, pups, handlers, leatherfolk, kinksters, educators, titleholders, and curious newcomers gather to learn, play, connect, and celebrate the community we share.

If we met this weekend, there's a good chance it was:

- at a class
- around the resort
- at a social event
- cheering on contestants
- talking after a workshop
- or while you were trying to figure out how my hand just opened a website on your phone

One of the things I love most about Dog Days is that everyone arrives with a different story.

Some people are attending their first event.

Some have been part of the community for decades.

Both belong here.

You can learn more about SEPA and Dog Days at:

https://sepetplay.org
