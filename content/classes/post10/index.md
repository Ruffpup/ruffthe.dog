# Chaos Corgi's Gunge Lab

What happens when adults are given permission to stop being composed for a little while?

Chaos Corgi's Gunge Lab is an exploration of messy play through the lenses of sensory experience, negotiated vulnerability, emotional release, and joyful chaos. While slime, foam, and other messy materials may be the tools, the real focus is on the experiences they create and the connections they foster.

Together we'll explore why messy play resonates with so many people, from sensory seekers and pups to kinksters interested in vulnerability, transformation, humiliation-adjacent dynamics, or simply the freedom that comes from embracing playfulness without judgment.

Participants will learn about negotiation, consent, safety, scene design, cleanup strategies, and the emotional impact of allowing ourselves to be messy, silly, and fully present in the moment. Through discussion and interactive demonstrations, we'll examine how the same physical activity can create dramatically different experiences depending on intention, communication, and emotional tone.

Whether you're curious about slime, interested in sensory play, or simply wondering why anyone would voluntarily climb into a kiddie pool full of fluorescent gunge, this class offers a welcoming and beginner-friendly introduction to one of kink's most playful forms of expression.

## What You'll Learn

Participants will learn:

- The fundamentals of gunge and messy play
- Why sensory chaos can be emotionally rewarding
- Negotiation and consent strategies for messy scenes
- Safety considerations and risk management
- Scene planning, containment, and cleanup techniques
- How emotional tone shapes the experience of play
- Ways to integrate messy play into existing dynamics and interests

## Who Should Attend?

This class is designed for curious beginners, pups and handlers, sensory play enthusiasts, dominants, submissives, and anyone interested in exploring messy play safely.

No prior experience is required.

Bring curiosity.

Leave your fear of getting messy at the door.
